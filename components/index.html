There's always room for improvement, and we are committed to learning and improving. We plan on continuously finding more ways to
reduce our impact on the planet.

All our products are 100% made in Italy, so we can keep our carbon footprint minimal while supporting local business.

In 2020, we achieved our first sustainability goal by eliminating plastic from all secondary packaging, including delivery.

In March 2021, we became a carbon-neutral company by offsetting our CO2 emissions with investments in reforestation in countries including
Cambodia, Peru, and Kenya.
